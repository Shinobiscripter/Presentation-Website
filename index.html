What is the $8.7B Bond?

The $8.7 billion investment helps modernize schools, labs, and technology to support Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills (TEKS).

Science Class Improvements

New labs, updated equipment, and safer learning spaces allow students to perform real experiments aligned with TEKS standards.

Technology & Innovation

Funding supports computers, simulations, and digital tools that improve scientific analysis and hands-on learning.

Why It Matters

Better resources = better understanding of biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science for DISD students.
